💡 What does Tone of Voice do?
It tells the AI how to write—not just what to write. The same topic can sound academic, punchy, friendly, or authoritative purely based on your tone instruction. It shapes sentence rhythm, vocabulary, and personal-devity throughout the entire article.
📝 Best practices
  • Describe a persona — "like explaining to a smart friend"
  • Set sentence-level rules — short punchy lines, use contractions
  • List words/phrases to avoid — "revolutionary", "game-changer"
  • Add a writing example for the AI to mimic
  • State the opinion stance — opinionated or neutral?
